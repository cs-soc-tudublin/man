# **ALUMNI** - Our Gracious Benefactors

Sometimes alumni of CS++'s committee may want to do an event.

This section covers some common events run by our alumni as well as how to contact them.

# Payment

Traditionally, we will pay our alumni the workshop rate (€35/h for up to 2 hours, as of March 2026). This is not a requirement, but instead done as a way to 'give back' for their year(s) of volunteering with CS++.

Not all alumni will take the money, or will instead use it for the event (I.e. food, materials, etc.), do not forget to speak to them about payment!
