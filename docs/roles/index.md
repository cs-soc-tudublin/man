# **ROLES** @ CS++

This section covers all roles within CS++.

There are three 'groups' of roles in CS++:

1. The **Core Committee**
	1. Chairperson
	2. Vice Chair
	3. Treasurer
	4. Secretary
	5. Health & Safety Officer
	6. Public Relations Officer
2. The **General Committee**
	1. Technical Events Officer
	2. Social Events Officer
	3. Second Year Rep
	4. First Year Rep
	5. Events Rep
3. The **Systems Administrators**
	1. 5x Systems Administrators

# Required Roles

CS++ is **REQUIRED** by TU Dublin to have the following 6 roles:

- Chairperson
- Treasurer
- Secretary
- Health & Safety Officer
- Public Relations Officer
- First Year Rep

# Elections

The first 5 mandatory roles (Chair - PRO) are usually elected in an AGM at the end of an academic year, with the First Year Rep being elected early in Semeter 1 the following year
